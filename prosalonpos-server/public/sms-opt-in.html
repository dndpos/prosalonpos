SMS Opt-In & Privacy

ProSalonPOS is a point-of-sale and appointment platform used by salons and barbershops. Salons using ProSalonPOS may send text messages (SMS) to their customers using a phone number operated by ProSalonPOS on behalf of the salon.

How consent is collected

A customer provides their phone number to their salon in one of the following ways:

  • In person at the front desk, when booking or checking in for an appointment.
  • Online, through the salon's booking page hosted by ProSalonPOS.
  • When the customer asks the salon to add or update their contact information.

By providing a mobile phone number to a salon that uses ProSalonPOS, the customer agrees to receive SMS text messages from that salon related to their appointments and account.

What messages customers may receive

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders.
  • Appointment changes, cancellations, or rescheduling.
  • Ready-for-checkout or "your stylist is ready" notifications.
  • Receipts and payment confirmations.
  • Occasional service updates or promotions from the salon the customer visits.
Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

How to opt out

Customers can stop receiving text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message they receive. They will receive a one-time confirmation that they have been unsubscribed and will receive no further SMS messages from that salon through ProSalonPOS.

For help, customers may reply HELP to any message, or contact their salon directly.

Privacy

Phone numbers collected for SMS are used only to communicate with the customer about their salon visits and account. ProSalonPOS does not sell customer phone numbers, and phone numbers are not shared with third parties except the SMS delivery provider (Twilio) strictly for message delivery. Mobile information and opt-in data are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
