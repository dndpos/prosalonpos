ProSalonPOS

Salon point of sale, built for front-desk life.

ProSalonPOS is an all-in-one platform that helps salons and barbershops book appointments, check in clients, ring up services, and keep customers informed — all from one place.

What ProSalonPOS does

ProSalonPOS is software used by salon and barbershop owners to run the front of their business. It handles scheduling, walk-ins, check-in, service tickets, payments, receipts, reports, staff time-tracking, memberships, gift cards, and customer communication.

Salons using ProSalonPOS can send appointment confirmations, reminders, and service updates to their clients by text message (SMS). See our SMS opt-in and privacy policy for details on how consent is collected and how customers can opt out.

Features

Appointment calendar

Book, reschedule, and track appointments across multiple staff members and stations.

Online booking portal

A public booking page so clients can schedule their own appointments.

Point of sale

Fast checkout, tips, discounts, gift cards, packages, and receipts.

Client records

Customer history, preferences, loyalty points, and membership tracking.

Staff & payroll

Time clock, commission tracking, and payroll-ready reports.

Text notifications

Appointment confirmations, reminders, and ready-for-checkout alerts via SMS.

Who it's for

  • Independent hair and nail salons.
  • Barbershops with multiple stations.
  • Day spas and lash / brow studios.
  • Multi-location salon groups.
